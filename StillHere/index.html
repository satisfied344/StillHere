how it works

write it down.
someone sits with you.

no account, no real name, no fixing. write the thing you can't say out loud, and a real person will be with it.

  1. 1

    write the heavy thing

    anonymously — a sentence, half a thought, or a whole story. whatever's sitting on your chest.

  2. 2

    ask for presence, not advice

    flip on "no advice" and readers know to just be with you — no "have you tried…".

  3. 3

    someone says "i'm here"

    a quiet reply, or just a heart — proof a real person read every word.

a quiet promise.

  • anonymous by default — no email, no real name, nothing to sign.
  • every post & comment is read for safety before it ever appears.
  • no-advice mode keeps your story safe from unwanted "fixes".
