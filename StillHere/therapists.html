— talk to a real person

find someone to listen.

a handful of trusted partners who do this for a living — counselors, psychologists, peer listeners. book directly with them, on their schedule, in their tone. StillHere doesn't sit in the middle: no fees, no booking system to fight, no data handled by us.

— what this is
a referral, not a marketplace.

we don't process payments, run video calls, or store sessions. each partner has their own scheduling and policies — clicking «book» opens their calendar in a new tab.

in crisis right now? please don't wait.

partner therapists schedule days in advance. if it feels urgent — please call a hotline that can talk to you in the next few minutes, free, 24/7.

— 01

how it works

three steps, no account needed
01
pick someone

read the short bios below. look at the languages they speak and what they help with most. trust your first instinct — you can always try a different partner.

02
book on their site

their «book» button opens their own scheduling page (Calendly, Doxy.me, or similar). you pick a slot, pay them directly, and they confirm via email.

03
meet them, calmly

video, voice, or text — whatever they offer. no recording, no notes shared with us. it's between you and them.

— 03

how we choose them

small list, on purpose
credentials
verified license

we check certificates, registry numbers, and references before listing anyone. no «life coaches», no unsupervised practice.

privacy
we don't see your session

booking, payment, and the conversation itself happen on their side. StillHere doesn't store, log, or sit in the middle.

pricing
no platform fee

you pay them directly. we take nothing. most partners offer a sliding scale — ask if cost is a concern.

a real person can listen differently.
