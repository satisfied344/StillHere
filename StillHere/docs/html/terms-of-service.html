The No Advice Rule

StillHere allows users to tag their posts with "No Advice". This is a core community value, not just a label.

When a post is tagged "No Advice", responding with advice, solutions, or suggestions – even well-intentioned ones – violates the author's explicitly stated wishes and our Community Guidelines. Repeated violations may result in account restrictions.

Sometimes people need to be heard, not fixed. Respecting this tag is how we honour that.
