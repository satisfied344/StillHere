Crisis Resources

You are not alone. If you are in pain right now – there are people ready to listen, right now, for free.

In immediate danger? Call your local emergency number – 112 (Europe), 911 (US/Canada), 999 (UK), 103 (Ukraine) – or ask someone nearby for help.
