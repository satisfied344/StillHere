— things you couldn't say

quiet letters.

short notes to people who'll never read them. an ex, a parent, the version of you that didn't make it through. write what you couldn't send, choose a colour for the weight of it, and leave it here.

— how this works

no replies. no support count. no responses. just somewhere to put it down.

  • name or word, not a full name
  • kept anonymous, like everything else here
  • read others when you need to feel less alone

inspired by The Unsent Project by Rora Blue — a project we love and learned from.
